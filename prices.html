Equipment Package

If you don't have your own equipment, you can hire some of ours. You'll get:

  • An Automatic Electric Gun (AEG)
  • A high capacity magazine (plus extra one for £2.50)
  • 1000 BBs
  • A set of camouflage overalls
  • A facemask or pair of goggles
  • A helmet
  • An assault vest or webbing belt

The only thing we don't supply is footwear. We recommend that you wear walking boots because our site is a woodland area, and there are trip hazards under foot.

Club Membership

If you have played 3 games with us in the space of 2 months, you can become a member of OTT. Membership costs £50 for your first year, and £40 to renew after that. We also offer a student discount and an under 16 discount.

Why become a member? Members get:

  • UKARA registration
  • Discounted game fees
  • Between 10% and 20% discount in the shop
  • Free gun rental, if your gun doesn't work
