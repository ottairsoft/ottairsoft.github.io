From The A55 North Wales Expressway

If you're coming from the A55:

  1. Cross the Britania Bridge and come off at junction 8 onto the A5052 towards Benllech/Amlwch;
  2. Stay on the A5052 all the way to Brynrefail;
  3. Take the first left - just before the craft shop, with the yellow sign;
  4. Follow the road for about 1 miles;
  5. Take the first right, opposite the B7B called Too Hoots.
  6. Follow the road for approximatly 600 yards, and take the a right onto a dirt track
  7. After 300 Yards there will be gate on your right that is the entrance to the site
  8. Follow the site track down to the parking area.
