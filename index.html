About Us

Over The Top Airsoft Adventures (OTT for short) is a UKARA registered airsoft site in Anglesey, North Wales.

We have a large 120 acre woodland area split into 12 different areas. As well as that, we have a number of buildings - some of which have multiple floors - with lots of rooms to clear and places to hide.

There is free parking available onsite. As well as a shop where you can buy refreshments and army surplus gear, which will be open at the end of the year.

Whether you like outdoor games or close quarter battle, you'll have a great time at OTT!
